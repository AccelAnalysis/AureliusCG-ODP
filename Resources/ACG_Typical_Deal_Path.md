## Owner Requirements & Expectations

### Documentation & Responsiveness
- **Basic Intake:** Asset address, type, size, owner contact, objective, and timeline.  
- **Financials:** Rent roll, trailing 12-month operating statement, tax bill/assessment.  
- **Condition Docs:** Recent photos, tenant leases, maintenance records.  
- **Responsiveness:** Reply to emails/calls within 24-48 hours during active stages.  
- **Exclusivity:** Honor NDA and work exclusively with introduced partners during process.  

### What Owners Can Expect
- Transparent communication on progress and next steps.  
- Protection from unqualified or inappropriate inquiries.  
- Professional packaging that positions your asset for maximum value.  
- No fees until successful close (success-based compensation).

## ACG vs. Partner Roles

### What ACG Does
- **Qualification & Screening:** Ensure deals meet partner criteria and regulatory standards.  
- **Packaging:** Create compelling Asset Briefs and data rooms.  
- **Partner Matching:** Introduce only to pre-qualified, interested partners.  
- **Process Management:** Coordinate timelines, manage communications, enforce boundaries.  
- **Compliance:** Maintain regulatory compliance and protect all parties.  

### What Partners Do
- **Due Diligence:** Independent verification of all information.  
- **Underwriting:** Analyze financials, assess risks, determine valuation.  
- **Negotiation:** Direct discussions with owner on terms and price.  
- **Closing:** Handle legal, funding, and transaction completion.
